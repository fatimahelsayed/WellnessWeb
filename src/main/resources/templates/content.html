Bipolar disorder

Dealing With Depressive and Manic Episodes

Bipolar disorder is a complex mental health condition characterized by extreme mood swings that include emotional highs (mania or hypomania) and lows (depression). These mood episodes can vary in intensity and duration, often causing significant disruptions in daily life, relationships, and functioning. Individuals with bipolar disorder may experience periods of heightened energy, euphoria, and impulsivity during manic episodes, followed by profound sadness, hopelessness, and lethargy during depressive episodes.
